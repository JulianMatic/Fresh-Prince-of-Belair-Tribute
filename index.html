Philip Banks

Philip Banks is the Tritagonist on The Fresh Prince Of Bel-Air. He is the father of Hilary Banks, Carlton Banks, Ashley Banks, and Nicky Banks, the uncle of Will Smith, and the husband of Vivian Banks. He is portrayed by James Avery.

Vivian Banks

Vivian Banks is Will Smith's maternal aunt. She is portrayed by Janet Hubert during the first two seasons and the majority of the third, and by Daphne Maxwell Reid for the balance of the series. She is the Teteragonist of the series.
